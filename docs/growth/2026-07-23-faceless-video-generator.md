# 2026-07-23 - Faceless video generator acquisition page

## Objective

Capture high-intent organic searches from creators who have an idea but no source footage, then carry that exact topic through signup into the first Fast render and recurring checkout attribution.

## Search evidence

Current search results for `faceless video generator from prompt` consistently describe a complete workflow built from a topic: script, voiceover, visuals, captions, and vertical export. That is distinct from long-video clippers and individual text-to-video clip models.

Representative current results include:

- `shortsfaceless.com`
- `short.ai/faceless-video`
- `gofaceless.ai`
- `faceless-video.net`
- `shortskit.ai/tools/faceless-video-generator`

Kineo already had pages for free AI Shorts, text-to-video Shorts, niche ideas, and competitors, but no canonical page targeting the exact `faceless video generator` intent.

## PUSH #66

PUSH #66 adds `/faceless-video-generator` with:

- A topic-first headline and form above the fold.
- Honest free terms: up to three watermarked Fast videos every 24 hours, no card.
- Exact paid entry price and renewal: $4.90 first month, then $9.90/month.
- A real Kineo output preview.
- A comparison between Kineo, long-video clippers, and individual AI clip models.
- FAQPage, HowTo, SoftwareApplication, and VideoObject structured data.
- Canonical metadata, Open Graph video metadata, sitemap discovery, and internal links.
- Campaign `push66_faceless_video_generator` carried through signup and checkout.

## Measurement

The `push66FacelessVideoGenerator` block measures:

- Landing sessions.
- CTA clicks and topic submissions.
- New signups.
- First-render autostart eligibility and dispatch.
- Generation starts and completed first videos.
- Pricing views, checkout attempts, checkout starts, Stripe sessions, and recurring customers.

## Success gate

Review after the first 100 qualified landing sessions or 14 days, whichever comes first:

- Topic submission rate: at least 8%.
- Signup rate: at least 5%.
- First-video completion among signups: at least 50%.
- Checkout attempt among completed first-video users: at least 15%.
- At least one attributable active or trialing subscription.

If search impressions grow but visits do not, revise title and description. If visitors arrive but do not submit a topic, revise the proof and hero. If first videos complete but checkout does not start, revise the post-video clean-export offer rather than the acquisition page.
